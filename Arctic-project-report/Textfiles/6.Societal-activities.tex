\section{The Climate Impact on Societal Activities}
During the last few decades, the Arctic has experienced significant changes in its climate, which have had impacts on the societal activities in the region. During the last year interests in the Arctic have increased significantly, such as the opening of new shipping routes and resource extraction due to the retreat of the ice cap \citep{mager2009}. This has led to a growing geopolitical interest for major powers in the region, such as the United States, wanting more control over the area by obtaining Greenland \citep{CongressionalResearchService2026}. According to \citet{CongressionalResearchService2026} the interests in the Arctic are due to controlling new shipping routes that passes through Greenland, as well as getting access to new minerals and oil resources in the region. One can thus speculate that the retreat of the ice cap due to climate change has led to an increased interest in the Arctic, which has led to a growing geopolitical interest in the region. One can thus speculate how this will affect the people living in the Arctic, as well as the environment in the region.
