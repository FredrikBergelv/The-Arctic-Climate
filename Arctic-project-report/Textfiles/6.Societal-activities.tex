\section{The Climate Impact on Societal Activities}
Over the last decades, the Arctic has experienced significant changes in its climate, which have impacted societal activities in the region. Over the past year, interest in the Arctic has increased significantly, particularly in the opening of new shipping routes and resource extraction due to the retreat of the ice cap \citep{mager2009}. This has led to growing geopolitical interest among major powers in the region, such as the United States, which seeks greater control over the area, including potential efforts to obtain Greenland \citep{CongressionalResearchService2026}. According to \citet{CongressionalResearchService2026}, the interests in the Arctic stem from controlling shipping routes that pass through Greenland, as well as gaining access to new minerals and oil resources in the region, as previously mentioned. One can thus speculate about how this growing geopolitical interest will affect the people living in the Arctic, as well as the environment in the region.
